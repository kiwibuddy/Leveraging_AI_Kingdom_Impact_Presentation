Section 07

Ethical
Guardrails

The stochastic parrot problem, data privacy, and the 70/30 rule

Nathaniel Baldock

The Stochastic Parrot Problem

AI has no concept of truth. It predicts the next most plausible word. Plausible ≠ truthful.

!
Hallucination
AI fabricated an entire bibliography of academic papers that didn’t exist — with journal titles, dates, and page numbers.
!
Data Privacy
Pasting sensitive counseling emails into ChatGPT feeds confidential pastoral data into a public training corpus forever.
!
Doctrinal Drift
Generic AI doesn’t distinguish orthodox theology from heretical blog posts.
!
Authenticity
If AI writes your pastoral prayer, whose heart is speaking to God?
Nathaniel BaldockSources: Lausanne; Nigerian Pastoral Counsellors

The 70/30 Rule & Your AI Policy

70% — Your words, stories, tears, Spirit-led insights
30% — AI structure, research, data

🔒 Data Privacy

  • Never paste sensitive pastoral data into public AI
  • Compartmentalize counseling notes and prayer requests

⚠️ Bias Awareness

  • Public models carry cultural, political, theological biases of the internet
  • They are not neutral arbiters of truth

👁 Transparency

  • Be honest about when AI assists content creation
  • The contract of authenticity with your congregation matters
“Operating without an AI policy is like handing a teenager the keys to a sports car without teaching them the rules of the road.” — Jason Hamrock
Nathaniel BaldockSource: The Church Network / Jason Hamrock
